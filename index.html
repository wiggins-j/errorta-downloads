Errorta

Choose the models. Choose the team. Choose the goal.

Errorta combines local models and cloud models into one team. A PM agent leads coding agents - CLI subscriptions, local models on Ollama, and provider APIs - through rooms on your desktop to plan, build, review, and test. Built in tandem with the open-source AIAR framework for grounded answers.

One workspace. A whole software team.

Give Errorta a goal. A PM agent breaks it into tasks, assigns each one to a developer, reviewer, or tester, and drives the work through branches, pull requests, review, and real test runs - the way a small engineering team actually ships.

PM-guided teams

Plan, build, review, test.

A project manager agent decomposes your goal into a task backlog, assigns each task to the right role, re-plans as work lands or stalls, and holds the merge gate. You watch it work and steer at any point.

Local and cloud, one team

Cheapest capable model per task.

Mix Claude, Codex, and Cursor CLI subscriptions, local models on Ollama, and Anthropic / OpenAI / Google APIs in a single team. The PM picks the smallest model that can do each task and escalates to a stronger one only when it has to - and learns which models handle which work well.

Rooms, not chat windows

Configurable, inspectable, private.

Every agent works inside a room you configure - which models, what each one is allowed to see, how the work flows. Nothing leaves your machine unless you route it to a model that does, and every context hand-off is auditable.

Plug in the models you already have:

  • Codex CLI

  • Ollama - Qwen · Gemma · Mistral
  • Anthropic API
  • OpenAI API
  • Google API

The Council: many models, one answer.

Not every question is a coding task. Convene a Council - several models deliberating in a shared room across topologies you choose (parallel answers, round-robin, debate, moderator-led, blind review). Each member sees only what its policy allows, a finalizer or judge resolves the room, and every context hand-off is inspectable down to the byte. It is a second opinion engine that runs entirely on your terms.

Built on AIAR - grounded, and honest about it.

Errorta is built in tandem with AIAR, the open-source local-RAG framework that provides retrieval, LLM-as-judge evaluation, and a grounding / correction loop. Point your agents and Council at your own corpus and they answer from your data - with citations, a structured verdict on whether the answer holds up, and corrections that carry forward. Local AI that admits when it is wrong and remembers what you fixed.

More of what Errorta does.

Knowledge & grounding

Build a corpus from a plain-markdown brief, drag in your own documents, or point it at a folder to watch. Your agents and Council answer from that corpus with citations, a judge's verdict on whether the answer holds up, and corrections that carry forward.

Choose where it runs

Keep everything on your laptop, run models on your own machine over SSH, or reach for the cloud. You decide where your data and compute live - Errorta is local-capable, not locked to any one of them.

Carry it in your pocket

An iOS companion pairs with your desktop over your own network, so you can follow live runs, approve steps, and steer the team from your phone while the work happens at home.

A local AI backend

Other apps on your machine can call Errorta as their AI backend through a consent-gated local service API - your models, your data, behind one door you control.
